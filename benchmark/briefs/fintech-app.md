# Brief: fintech spend-management app

Feed this verbatim to `/prototype` (choose **Quick** mode), then score the output folder.

> Prototype a corporate spend-management app called **Ledgerline**. Tone: precise,
> corporate, minimal. Inspiration: Ramp and Mercury. Audience: internal stakeholder review.
> Scope: 4 screens — Dashboard (balance + recent transactions), Cards, Transactions
> (filterable), Approvals. Content: realistic placeholder — real-sounding merchants,
> plausible amounts and dates (recent).

Expected from a good run: 4 screens, density layouts (condensed / standard / roomy),
personas for admin vs employee, an empty approvals state, transactions with realistic
merchants and amounts (no "Example Corp", no far-future dates), inline form validation.
